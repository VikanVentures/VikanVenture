The Greeks - Option Trading


Duration: 10 Hrs

Course Overview: Dive into the world of options trading and learn to navigate the complexities with confidence. This course provides a structured approach to mastering the basics and advanced strategies of options trading, focusing on risk management and live analysis.

Course Content:

1. Introduction to Options

  • Understand the fundamentals of options trading
  • The terminology used, and how options differ from other financial instruments.

2. Moneyness of Options

  • In-the-Money (ITM)
  • At-the-Money (ATM)
  • Out-of-the-Money (OTM)
  • Their implications in trading decisions.

3. Intrinsic Value and Time Value

  • Explore the two main components of an option's price and how they impact trading strategies and profitability.

4. Open Interest with Volatility

  • Analyze market trends by understanding open interest
  • The role of volatility in option pricing and trading.

5. Basic Options Premium

  • Break down the factors influencing option premiums
  • How to calculate them for better decision-making.

6. Advanced Trading Strategies

  • Master advanced options strategies like straddles, strangles, spreads
  • Iron condors to maximize gains and minimize risks.

7. Greeks and Risk Management

  • Dive deep into the Greeks (Delta, Gamma, Theta, Vega, and Rho)
  • How to use them for effective risk management and optimization.

8. Live Position Analysis

  • Engage in practical, hands-on analysis of live market positions to solidify learning and apply theoretical concepts in real-time scenarios.
