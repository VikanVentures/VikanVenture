Advanced Technical Analysis


Duration: 20 Hrs

Course Overview:

The Advanced Technical Analysis course is designed for individuals aiming to enhance their skills in market analysis. This course delves deep into advanced concepts, equipping participants with the tools and techniques to analyze market trends, make informed decisions, and maximize trading success.

Course Content:

1. Candlestick Patterns

  • Learn to identify and interpret key candlestick patterns, such as doji, hammer, engulfing patterns, and their significance in predicting market movements.

2. Technical Analysis Concepts

  • Support and Resistance: Understand the importance of these levels in trading decisions.
  • Trend Lines: Learn to draw and analyze trend lines for identifying market directions.
  • Trend Channels: Discover how trend channels can provide insights into market trends.

3. Analyze Financial Statements of Companies

  • Develop skills to evaluate the financial health of companies by analyzing their income statements, balance sheets, and cash flow statements.

4. Financial Ratios

  • Activity Ratios: Measure how efficiently a company utilizes its assets.
  • Liquidity Ratios: Assess a company's ability to meet short-term obligations.
  • Solvency Ratios: Evaluate long-term financial stability and debt management.
  • Financial Ratios: Learn comprehensive ratios that provide insights into profitability, efficiency, and overall financial health.
